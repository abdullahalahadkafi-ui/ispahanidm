Privacy Policy

Transparency and Data Protection for Students and Parents

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official website of Ispahani Dakhil Madrasah (https://ispahanidm.onrender.com). We are an MPO-enrolled educational institution managed by the Ispahani Group. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle student, parent, and visitor information.

2. Information Collection & Usage

We collect minimal information required for academic purposes. This includes:

  • Academic Data: Student names, Roll numbers, and IDs used to display exam results and marksheets.
  • Inquiry Data: Information submitted via our contact forms (Name, Email, Phone) to respond to academic queries.
  • Log Files: We use standard log files that record IP addresses, browser types, and date stamps for administrative and security purposes.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure of student information. Academic results and personal details are handled with high confidentiality. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy Protection

Our website primarily serves students and children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children without parental consent. If a parent or guardian believes that we have such information, please contact us immediately to have it removed.

6. Legal Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling, please contact our administration office at:
